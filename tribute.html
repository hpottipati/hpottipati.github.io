Mahatma Gandhi

Born Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, Mahatma Gandhi was and Indian politician and activist who helped free India from British rule. He was known as Mahatma to followers, which means "Great Soul". He began activism once he saw mistreatment of Indians in South Africa.

Major Achievements:

  • The salt march - He led a march to protest the British rules that prevented Indian from obtaining salt by themselves. As a result, they had to buy it from the British with a high tax.
  • Fasted for 21 days to protest again the British. This allowed India to become independent in 1947
